The Story

Nova Core started with one frustration.

Too many brands claimed "premium" and delivered mediocre. Too many streetwear labels chased trends and left their identity behind. Nova Core was built as the answer to both.

We started in 2025 with a single hoodie and a clear brief: make something that doesn't need a logo to announce itself. Something you reach for first. Something that doesn't fall apart on the second wash.

Every piece since has followed the same logic. Materials chosen for quality, not margin. Silhouettes designed to outlive the season. Details considered once, then considered again.

Processing Times

All orders are processed within 1–3 business days of being placed. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will begin processing the next business day.

During drops or limited releases, processing may extend to 3–5 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking once your order has shipped.

Returns Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery on unworn, unwashed items with all original tags attached. Sale items and limited pieces are final sale.

  • Items must be in original condition — unworn, unwashed, no odours or stains
  • Original tags must be attached
  • Returns must include your order number and reason
  • Shipping costs for returns are the customer's responsibility unless we made an error
  • Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the return

Exchanges

We currently process exchanges as a return + new purchase. This ensures the item you want stays available. If you need a different size or colour, return your original item and place a new order. Contact us and we'll hold the item for you where possible.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you received a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us at hello@novacore.co within 7 days of delivery with your order number and photos. We'll make it right at no cost to you.

Customs & Duties (International)

International orders may be subject to import duties, taxes, or customs fees upon arrival. These charges are outside of our control and are the customer's responsibility. Nova Core is not responsible for delays caused by customs clearance.

Size Guide

Fit Notes

Most Nova Core pieces are cut with a relaxed streetwear silhouette. If you want a standard fit, size down one. If you prefer a true oversized look, keep your usual size.

Still unsure? Measure your favourite hoodie or tee at home and compare it against the chart below.

Tops Measurement Chart

How to Measure

  • Chest: Measure straight across the widest part of the garment and double it
  • Length: Measure from the highest shoulder point to the hem
  • Shoulder: Measure seam to seam across the top back panel
